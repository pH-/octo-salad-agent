Friday — Black Bean and Corn Salad with Lime Dressing

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: black beans

💡 Black beans soaked Saturday night, cooked Sunday
💡 Can prep all components in advance except avocado

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked black beans (from 1 1/4 cups dried black beans)
  • 2 ears fresh corn, kernels removed (or 1.5 cups frozen corn)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, diced
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped (seeds removed for mild)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 4 cups mixed greens or romaine
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • If using fresh corn, boil for 3-4 minutes, cool, and cut kernels off cob
  • Dice tomatoes, bell pepper, onion, jalapeño, and chop cilantro
  • Whisk together lime zest, lime juice, vinegar, olive oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper
  • Combine black beans, corn, and all vegetables (except avocado)
  • Toss with dressing, serve over greens, top with fresh avocado
