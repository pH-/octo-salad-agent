Friday — Lentil and Roasted Vegetable Salad with Lemon-Tahini Dressing

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: green or brown lentils

💡 Vegetables roasted fresh for optimal flavor; lentils and dressing prepped ahead

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked green or brown lentils (from 3/4 cup dried lentils)
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 small red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for roasting)
  • 5 cups mixed greens or spinach
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Toss cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and red onion with olive oil, salt, and pepper; roast at 425°F for 25 minutes
  • Arrange mixed greens on plates, top with cooked lentils and roasted vegetables
  • Drizzle with prepared lemon-tahini dressing and garnish with fresh parsley
