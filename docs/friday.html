Friday — Paneer Salad with Spicy Greek Yogurt Dressing

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: paneer

Substitution: Replace iceberg lettuce with romaine or mixed greens

Ingredients

  • 8 oz paneer, cubed
  • 1-2 teaspoons oil for pan-frying
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (or hung curd)
  • 2 tablespoons soaked cashews
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano or Italian herbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed bell peppers, diced
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Blend Greek yogurt, soaked cashews, garlic, spices, lemon juice, maple syrup, and salt until smooth
  • Heat oil in pan and pan-fry paneer cubes with salt until golden on all sides
  • Combine lettuce, bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and cucumber in large bowl
  • Top with pan-fried paneer and drizzle with yogurt dressing
  • Toss and serve
