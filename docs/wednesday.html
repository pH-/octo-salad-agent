Wednesday — Crispy Tofu Salad with Lemon Herb Dressing

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: tofu

💡 Tofu needs to be cooked night-of for best texture
💡 Can press tofu and prep vegetables in advance

Ingredients

  • 14 oz extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for tofu)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (for marinade)
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 English cucumber, sliced thin
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds, toasted
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (for dressing)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Press tofu for 20 minutes, cube and marinate in lemon juice, balsamic, maple syrup, garlic, and salt
  • Pan-fry marinated tofu in olive oil until golden and crispy on all sides
  • Chop romaine, slice cucumber, halve tomatoes
  • Make dressing by whisking together oil, lemon juice, herbs, maple syrup, salt, and pepper
  • Toss lettuce and vegetables with dressing, top with crispy tofu, avocado, and almonds
