Wednesday — Black Bean and Avocado Salad with Cilantro-Lime Vinaigrette

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: black beans

💡 Avocado diced fresh to prevent browning; everything else prepped ahead

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked black beans (from 3/4 cup dried black beans)
  • 5 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Chop romaine lettuce and place in large serving bowl
  • Add cooked black beans, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro
  • Dice avocado fresh, add to salad, drizzle with prepared dressing and toss gently
