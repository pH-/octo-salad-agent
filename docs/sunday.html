Sunday — Marinated Tofu and Arugula Salad with Ginger-Miso Dressing

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: tofu

💡 Tofu marinated and cooked fresh for best texture; vegetables and dressing prepped ahead

Ingredients

  • 14 oz extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for cooking)
  • 5 cups arugula
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons white miso paste
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar (for dressing)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (for dressing)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (for dressing)
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds for garnish

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Marinate pressed tofu cubes in soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, and sesame oil for 15 minutes
  • Pan-fry marinated tofu in olive oil over medium-high heat until golden and crispy, about 10 minutes
  • Combine arugula, red cabbage, carrots, and green onions; top with crispy tofu, drizzle with prepared ginger-miso dressing, and garnish with sesame seeds
