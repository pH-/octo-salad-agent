Sunday — Green Lentil Salad with Za'atar and Roasted Red Peppers

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: lentils and chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup dried green lentils, yields ~1.25 cups cooked
  • 1/2 cup dried chickpeas, yields ~1.25 cups cooked
  • 1 large cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup roasted red peppers (jarred), diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons za'atar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Cook lentils in salted boiling water until tender, about 20 minutes; drain and rinse in cold water
  • Combine cooked lentils, chickpeas, cucumber, roasted red peppers, red onion, and parsley in large bowl
  • Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, za'atar, salt, and pepper
  • Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine
  • Serve immediately or chill
