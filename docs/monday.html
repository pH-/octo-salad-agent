Monday — Mediterranean Chickpea Salad with Lemon-Dijon Dressing

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: chickpeas

💡 Chickpeas soaked Saturday night, cooked Sunday
💡 Dressing can be made in advance and stored separately

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas (from 3/4 cup dried chickpeas)
  • 1 English cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 3 cups mixed greens or arugula
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon sumac
  • 1/2 teaspoon Aleppo pepper or red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Dice cucumber, tomatoes, peppers, onion, and herbs
  • Whisk together dressing ingredients
  • Combine cooked chickpeas with vegetables in large bowl
  • Add mixed greens, toss with dressing, top with avocado
