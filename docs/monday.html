Monday — Tuscan White Bean Salad with Za'atar

Original recipe ↗ · Protein: white beans (cannellini)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried white beans (cannellini or great northern), yields ~2.5 cups cooked
  • 1 cup cucumbers, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon za'atar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sumac
  • 1/2 teaspoon Aleppo pepper (or red pepper flakes)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Tonight's steps (<10 min)

  • Combine cooked white beans, cucumbers, tomatoes, green onions, parsley, and mint in large bowl
  • Add lemon zest and season with salt, pepper, za'atar, sumac, and Aleppo pepper
  • Add lemon juice and olive oil, toss well to combine
  • Let sit 10 minutes before serving
