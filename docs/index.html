📋 Weekend prep

Saturday
  • Soak 1 cup dried white beans in water (for Monday) - Saturday 9pm
  • Soak 1/2 cup dried French green lentils in water (for Tuesday) - Saturday 9pm
  • Soak 1 cup dried chickpeas in water (for Tuesday and Thursday) - Saturday 9pm

Sunday
  • Cook white beans: drain, add to pot with 2 inches water, boil then simmer 60-90 minutes until tender; drain and cool
  • Cook French green lentils: drain, add to pot with garlic and olive oil, cover with 4 cups water, boil then simmer 20-35 minutes; drain and cool
  • Cook chickpeas: drain, add to pot with 2 inches water, boil then simmer 60-90 minutes until tender; drain and cool (divide into 2 portions for Tuesday and Thursday)
  • Cook kidney beans: drain, add to pot with 2 inches water, boil then simmer 60-90 minutes until tender; drain and cool
  • Cook green lentils: drain, add to pot with water, boil then simmer 20-30 minutes; drain and cool
  • Prep vegetables: wash and dry all salad greens, store in containers with paper towels
  • Chop vegetables: dice cucumbers (store in airtight containers), slice radishes, dice bell peppers
  • Chop herbs: parsley, mint, dill, cilantro (store separately in damp paper towels in containers)
  • Make dressing jars: prepare lemon-olive oil dressings for Monday, Thursday, Sunday (store refrigerated)
  • Toast nuts and seeds: almonds, sesame seeds for Saturday (store in airtight container)
  • Soak cashews for Friday's dressing (2 hours in warm water)
