📋 Weekend prep

Saturday
  • Sort and rinse dried chickpeas (3/4 cup), black beans (1 1/4 cups), and white beans (1 1/4 cups)
  • Place chickpeas in one bowl, black beans in another, white beans in a third
  • Cover each with water (at least 3 inches above beans)
  • Soak overnight (12 hours) at room temperature
  • Toast almonds (1/2 cup total) and sesame seeds (2 tablespoons) - store in airtight container
Sunday
  • COOK CHICKPEAS: Drain and rinse soaked chickpeas, place in pot with fresh water covering by 2 inches, bring to boil, reduce to simmer 60-90 minutes until tender, drain and cool (yields ~2 cups cooked)
  • COOK BLACK BEANS: Drain and rinse soaked black beans, place in pot with fresh water covering by 2 inches, bring to boil, reduce to simmer 60-90 minutes until tender, drain and cool (yields ~3 cups cooked)
  • COOK WHITE BEANS: Drain and rinse soaked white beans, place in pot with fresh water covering by 2 inches, bring to boil, reduce to simmer 60-90 minutes until tender, drain and cool (yields ~3 cups cooked)
  • Store all cooked beans separately in airtight containers in refrigerator
  • Make dressing for Monday (Mediterranean chickpea): whisk together 3 tbsp olive oil, 3 tbsp lemon juice, 2 cloves minced garlic, 1 tbsp Dijon, spices - store in jar
  • Wash and prep greens: wash 20 cups mixed greens/spinach/arugula/romaine total, spin dry, store in containers with paper towels
  • Prep aromatics: dice 1 large red onion (store in water), mince 10 cloves garlic total (store in olive oil)
  • Chop herbs in advance if desired: parsley, mint, basil, cilantro - wrap in damp paper towels, store in bags
  • Make additional dressings and store in jars (optional for time-saving)
